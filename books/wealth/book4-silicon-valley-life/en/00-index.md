## About This Book

This is Book 4 in the "Silicon Valley Forrest Gump" series.

The first three books covered investing, finances, and career.
This book covers life, parenting, faith, and community.

**Because life isn't just about money.**

## How to Read This Book

**If you're new to Silicon Valley:**
- Start with Chapter 27 to understand the Chinese community

**If you're raising kids:**
- Chapters 28-29 will save you money and anxiety

**If you're feeling anxious:**
- Chapter 30 is written for you

**If you're thinking about life's meaning:**
- Chapter 31 will point you in a direction

---

## Core Messages

1. **Church isn't just about faith** — It's community, support system, and life partners
2. **Parenting requires total cost accounting** — But anxiety costs more than money
3. **Extracurriculars: quality over quantity** — Depth beats breadth
4. **Success can be defined differently** — Health, happiness, meaning
5. **Community is real wealth** — Money runs out, people stay forever
