## About This Book / 关于本书

This book focuses on retirement planning for Silicon Valley tech workers. It covers everything from 401k basics to complex California real estate tax strategies.

本书专注于硅谷科技人员的退休规划。从401k基础到复杂的加州房产税策略，一应俱全。
