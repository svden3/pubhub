## Sun Tzu's Seven Calculations

Sun Tzu compared opposing forces through seven questions:

| Calculation | Original Meaning | Investment Mapping |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Which ruler has Dao** | Whose ruler has moral authority | Whose governance is better |
| **Which general has ability** | Whose general is more capable | Whose management is stronger |
| **Which has Heaven and Earth** | Who has timing and terrain | Whose environment is favorable |
| **Which has discipline** | Whose laws are enforced | Whose execution is better |
| **Which is stronger** | Whose army is more powerful | Who has more resources |
| **Which is better trained** | Whose soldiers are more skilled | Whose team is more professional |
| **Which rewards clearly** | Whose incentives are clearer | Whose incentive system is better |

### Case: Walmart vs Sears (2010)

| Metric | Walmart | Sears |
|--------|---------|-------|
