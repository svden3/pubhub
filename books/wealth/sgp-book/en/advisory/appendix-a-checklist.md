# Appendix A: Investment Decision Checklist

> Before every investment, go through this checklist.

---

## Pre-Investment Check

### 1. Basic Conditions ✓

- [ ] I have 6+ months of emergency savings
- [ ] I have no high-interest debt (credit cards, etc.)
- [ ] This is "extra money" I won't need for 5 years
- [ ] I've set up automatic investing

### 2. Understanding Check ✓

- [ ] I can explain in three sentences how this investment makes money
- [ ] I know how much I could lose if I'm wrong
- [ ] I'm not buying because "everyone else is buying"
- [ ] I'm not intimidated by "complex" terminology

### 3. Emotion Check ✓

- [ ] I'm not selling in a panic
- [ ] I'm not buying in a frenzy
- [ ] This is a planned decision, not an impulse
- [ ] I don't have FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

### 4. Diversification Check ✓

- [ ] This investment is no more than 10% of total assets
- [ ] I don't own (much) company stock
- [ ] My portfolio is diversified
- [ ] I have stocks, bonds, and cash allocation

## Sell Check

Before clicking "Sell":

| Question | If "yes," maybe don't sell |
|----------|---------------------------|
| Am I selling because the price dropped? | ⚠️ |
| Am I selling because news scared me? | ⚠️ |
| Am I selling to lock in gains? | ⚠️ |
| Am I selling because friends are selling? | ⚠️ |

**Valid reasons to sell:**
- [ ] I need to use this money
- [ ] The investment thesis fundamentally changed
- [ ] There's a better investment opportunity

## Crisis Check

When the market drops significantly (> 20%):

1. **Don't open your account to look at numbers**
2. **Don't watch financial news**
3. **Ask yourself these questions:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Is this temporary panic or a real problem? | _________________ |
| Are the companies I hold still making money? | _________________ |
| Do I have cash to buy more? | _________________ |
| Can I handle another 30% drop? | _________________ |

**If company fundamentals haven't changed, don't sell.**
