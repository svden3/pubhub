# Chapter 20: Ivy League Isn't the Only Path

> "Mama said: There are many roads to success. Ivy League is just one of them, and it's the most crowded."

---

## The Silicon Valley Anxiety

In Silicon Valley, there's a disease called "Ivy anxiety."

Symptoms:
- Planning the "Ivy path" from kindergarten
- Paying $500/hour for college consultants
- Making kids learn 10 instruments and 5 sports
- Editing your kid's application essay at 2 AM

Final result:

**Ivy acceptance rate < 5%.**

**95% of kids will "fail."**

But did they really fail?

## The Numbers

### Ivy vs State School

| School | 4-Year Cost | Post-Graduation Salary |
|--------|-------------|------------------------|
| Harvard | $320,000 | $90,000 starting |
| UC Berkeley | $120,000 (in-state) | $85,000 starting |
| Difference | $200,000 | $5,000/year |

**How many years to earn back the $200,000 difference?**

## What Really Matters

### 1. Major > School Ranking

| Major | State School Salary | Ivy Salary |
|-------|---------------------|------------|
| Computer Science | $110,000 | $120,000 |
| Sociology | $45,000 | $50,000 |

**A CS grad from a state school earns more than a sociology grad from Ivy.**

### 2. Skills > Degrees

What employers value most:
1. Problem-solving ability
2. Teamwork
3. Continuous learning
4. Practical project experience

**Degrees are just the door opener. Skills are the moat.**

### 3. Network Can Be Built Later

"The value of Ivy is the network."

This is true. But networks can also be built at work.

Sarah spent 16 years at Google. Her network is stronger than any Ivy's.

---

## Smarter Paths

### Path 1: Top State School + Save Money

Go to UC Berkeley / UCLA / UT Austin / Georgia Tech.

Save the $200,000 difference.

Use that money to start a business, invest, or get a graduate degree.

### Path 2: Community College → Transfer

California community college → UC system transfer.

2 years community college: $10,000
2 years UC Berkeley: $60,000
Total: $70,000

**Same degree, one-third the price.**

### Path 3: Technical Route

Coding Bootcamp: $15,000-20,000
Training time: 3-6 months
Starting salary: $80,000-120,000

**4 years vs 6 months. $300,000 vs $20,000.**

## For Parents

1. **Don't pass your anxiety to your kids**
   - Your face shouldn't become your child's pressure

2. **Success has many definitions**
   - Money? Happiness? Meaningful work?
   - Ivy doesn't guarantee any of them

3. **The best investment is presence**
   - Spending $500,000 on consultants vs spending 1 hour with your child daily
   - The latter has higher returns

## Mama's Words
